Registration

Registration fees

The registration fees cover attending both TFP and TFPIE, and are as follows:
  • The full fee is 3700 SEK (excl. VAT)
  • The student fee is 2600 SEK (excl. VAT)
Both fees include participation in the social dinner. Additional tickets for the social dinner may be purchased for 800 SEK (excl. VAT). Please read the notice on taxation rules below.

Important Notice On Taxation

  • In accordance with Swedish Value Added Tax (VAT) requirements, any company or organization from EU with a registered VAT number or equivalent tax registration number is NOT required to pay Swedish VAT for registration fee attending the conference in Sweden.
  • Any company or organization outside EU with a registered tax registration number, or a valid company name is NOT required to pay Swedish VAT for registration fee attending the conference in Sweden.
  • Delegates from Sweden, as well as delegates from EU who do not provide a VAT or equivalent tax identification number, are required to pay VAT.
  • Private persons (i.e. not employed, not students) are required to pay VAT.
