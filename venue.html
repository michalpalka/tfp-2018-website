Getting to Gothenburg

Flights: Gothenburg is served by an international airport Göteborg Landvetter Airport (IATA code: GOT). It is Sweden's second largest airport with many daily flights from all of the major hubs in Europe.

From airport to hotel by taxi: Such a taxi ride is likely to cost around 450-550 SEK. All taxis accept card payments, and offer fixed price from the airport to the centre. We recommed checking the price with your driver before entering the taxi, as prices may vary between companies. Reputable taxi companies in Gothenburg include: Taxi Göteborg and Taxi Kurir.

From airport to hotel by bus: One can catch the Flygbussarna airport coach from outside the check-in hall of the airport. This coach stops at the central station in Gothenburg (Nils Ericson Terminalen), and at the Korsvägen bus stop
