Trends in Functional Programming 2018 in Gothenburg, Sweden

11-13 June 2018

  • Call for Papers

The symposium on Trends in Functional Programming (TFP) is an international forum for researchers with interests in all aspects of functional programming, taking a broad view of current and future trends in the area. It aspires to be a lively environment for presenting the latest research results, and other contributions. Authors of papers have the choice of having their contributions formally reviewed either before or after the Symposium. Both the pre-symposium and the post-symposium refereeing process will select a subset of the articles for formal publication.

TFP 2018 will be the main event of a pair of functional programming events. TFP 2018 running from 11 to 13 June will be accompanied by the International Workshop on Trends in Functional Programming in Education (TFPIE), which will take place on 14 June.

The events will take place at the Chalmers Campus Johanneberg in Gothenburg.
